Process

WHAT I DO

Discover

I start by understanding the project goals, target audience, and brand identity. Through research and inspiration, I identify the best creative direction before beginning the design process.

Ideate

I brainstorm ideas, sketch concepts, create mood boards, and explore layouts, colors, typography, and visual styles to develop effective design solutions.

Design

I transform concepts into polished visuals by focusing on usability, aesthetics, and consistency. Whether it's a mobile app, website, branding, or marketing material, every detail is crafted with purpose.

Deliver

I review, test, and refine every design based on feedback to ensure a high-quality final outcome. The completed files are optimized and delivered for both digital and print platforms.

Branding

LOGOS & BRANDING

A professional overview detailing three custom visual identity frameworks. Each project explains the design concept, typographic decisions, color choice rationale, and strategic process behind the mark.

01 / Artisanal Coffee Mark

For this brand identity, the goal was to communicate warmth and handcrafted quality. The mark merges organic geometries with custom hand-lettered details. We wanted to escape standard corporate layouts to capture an authentic, independent coffee culture. The process started with hand sketches before vector refinement, ensuring the line quality retained a tactile feel.

Color Palette

Warm Espresso (#3c2517), Cream, and subtle accents of organic Ochre.

Typography

Custom hand-drawn letters combined with Space Mono as a functional subhead.

Design Thinking

Retaining natural irregularities to emphasize artisanal and handcrafted values.

Process

Hand sketching, digital plotting, packaging print checks, and scale testing.

02 / Modern Package Mark

Designed for a lifestyle packaging line, this visual system emphasizes clean geometry and structural legibility. The mark is built with sharp angles and clean borders, allowing it to scale effortlessly down to tiny tags or up to shipping containers. We balanced the minimalism by using soft color systems, giving the mark an approachable and modern feel.

Color Palette

Beige Light (#e6dfd7), Stark Charcoal, and Crimson Wine (#9C3D45).

Typography

Inter in varying font weights, keeping a structured layout.

Design Thinking

Maximum legibility at small sizes, ensuring clean application on cardboard boxes.

Process

Geometric grids, 3D product mockup checks, and high-contrast color validation.

03 / Bespoke Creative System

For this identity, the focus was to represent multi-dimensional design practices (UI, branding, and graphics). The mark utilizes clean overlapping abstract shapes to create depth. The identity functions as a dynamic system that shifts colors depending on the context, while keeping the core structural lines recognizable.

Color Palette

Stark Ink black (#000000), Pure White, and warm terracotta accents.

Typography

Playfair Display for elegant editorial subheads, with Space Mono for tech details.

Design Thinking

Visualizing overlap and multi-disciplinary methods through abstract shape unions.

Process

Dynamic variable design systems, responsive screens tests, and print collateral review.
